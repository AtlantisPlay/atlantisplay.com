Who we are

AtlantisPlay, a brand of AtlantisTeam Development Ltd, is a game development company founded in 2019 by an already established Team of seasoned game development professionals.

With headquarters in Cyprus and locations in Israel and Ukraine, we are capable of performing full game development cycle, including client-side and server-side development, art, animations, sounds, project management, and quality assurance of any game.

Our team members have 10+ years in the industry, having worked at big names like Wargaming, Playtech, and Playtika. Most of our developers are senior-level professionals with 15+ years of coding under their belts.
