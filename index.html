Who we are

AtlantisPlay, a brand of AtlantisTeam Development Ltd, is a gaming company founded in 2019 by an already established Team of game development professionals.

With headquarters in Cyprus and locations in Israel and Ukraine, we are capable of performing full game development cycle, including client-side and server-side development, art, animations, sounds, project management, and quality assurance of any game.

Our Team consists of people with more than 10 years of experience in Game Dev, who previously worked in companies like Wargaming, Playtech, Playtika and others. The absolute majority of our developers are Senior Developers with more than 15 years of experience in the field.
